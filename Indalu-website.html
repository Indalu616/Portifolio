    My name is Indalu Kelbesa. I was born in the remote reaches of Ethiopia, nestled far from the bustle of the capital city, Addis Ababa. Born into a world where opportunities were scarce, but dreams were vast, my journey has been one of unwavering determination and resilience.

    From a young age, I harbored ambitions far beyond the boundaries of my rural upbringing. Despite the challenges of limited resources and access to quality education, I committed myself to achieving my goals. This determination bore fruit when I achieved remarkable success in the Grade 8 regional examination, a pivotal moment that paved the path for my future endeavors.

    The turning point in my educational journey came with the opportunity to join the Oromia Development Association (ODA) Special Boarding School. It was a steppingstone towards realizing my potential, providing me with the environment and resources necessary to thrive academically. I seized this chance with fervor, recognizing it as a gateway to a brighter future.

    My ascent continued with my outstanding performance in the Grade 12 Ethiopian National Examination. Despite the odds stacked against me, I emerged triumphant, proving that perseverance and dedication can overcome any obstacle. My strengths in mathematics and problem-solving propelled me forward, laying the groundwork for my future pursuits. Now I am a web developer, progammer and freelancer.
